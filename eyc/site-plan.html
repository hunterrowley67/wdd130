Overview

Purpose

The purpose is to create a website to inform customers about the skill and talents of my sister Elizabeth so they can purchase her creations. It is to expand her reach and bring potential customers to her so she can increase her sales.

Audience

The target audience are people looking for homemade gifts and products. It is also targeted towards people who enjoy crocheted items but don't have the skills to make them for themselves.
